ArtWalk Deep Ellum

Archival project • 1985–1997
Overview

ArtWalk Deep Ellum was an independent art festival held in Dallas, Texas from 1985 to 1997. It contributed to the development of Deep Ellum’s creative and cultural identity during that period.

Archive

This project is dedicated to documenting and preserving the history of ArtWalk Deep Ellum through photographs, flyers, printed materials, and community contributions.

It serves as an evolving public record intended to organize and protect historical materials related to the event and its cultural impact.

Status

This archive is currently under development. Materials are actively being collected and organized.

Contributions, materials, and historical references are welcome as this archive grows.
